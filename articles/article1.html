Clinical Case Study: Hypertension

Author: Dr. K. Nandhakumar | Pharm.D
Published: 2026

Patient Details

A 55-year-old male with a history of hypertension presented with uncontrolled blood pressure.

Clinical Assessment

Blood pressure readings showed persistent elevation despite monotherapy.

Pharmacological Management

Combination therapy including ACE inhibitors and lifestyle modification was advised.

Conclusion

Clinical pharmacist intervention improved therapeutic outcomes and adherence.
